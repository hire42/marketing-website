10 website concepts to review

Ten different homepage directions for hire42.co.nz — same brand, same copy, ten distinct looks. Open each, then vote on the one that feels most like Hire42.

Option 01

Classic & Clean

Calm app-store style. Eggshell canvas, brand green, phone hero. The safe, professional default.
Option 02

Bold & Industrial

Dark, rugged, high-contrast. Heavy display type with tango-orange accents. Speaks tradie.
Option 03

Split Path

Two audiences, one screen. Lender side and renter side, split down the middle. Choose your lane.
Option 04

Warm & Editorial

Magazine-style warmth. Wheat tones, big headlines, generous columns. Premium but grounded.
Option 05

Minimal & Swiss

Whitespace and grid discipline. Mostly type, one green accent. Confident and uncluttered.
Option 06

Marketplace

Listings front and centre. A search bar and a grid of real gear, like the product itself.
Option 07

App Showcase

The app is the hero. Phone frames on teal halos, gradient sections, download-led.
Option 08

Conversion Landing

Long, punchy, single column. Proof, FAQ, and a sticky download bar. Built to convert.
Option 09

Vibrant Gradient

Green-to-teal gradients, soft glass cards, rounded everything. Modern and approachable.
Option 10

Kiwi & Local

Proudly Far North. Map motif, earthy tones, local-trade story. Community without the cliché.
