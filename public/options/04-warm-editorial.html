Idle equipment is leaving money on the table.

Your excavator, water blaster, scaffolding, trailer or compressor earns nothing when it's parked. Hire42 is the marketplace where Kiwi businesses and tradespeople hire kit out to each other — so the gear that's sitting in the yard finally earns its keep.

You stay in control

Accept or decline every rental request. Set your own price and availability.

Paid straight to your bank

Funds go to you after each rental, through Stripe. No invoices to chase.

Hire from people who use it

Equipment that's maintained and job-ready, from operators near you.

“Got a piece of gear sitting in the yard doing nothing? Put it to work. List it in minutes, set your own price, and earn while you're not using it.”

The Hire42 idea, in one line

For lenders

Earn from what you already own.

  1. List your equipment with photos and a daily rate.
  2. Accept rental requests from verified renters.
  3. Hand over the gear and get paid.

For renters

The right kit, without buying it.

  1. Search for equipment near you.
  2. Select your dates and pay securely in the app.
  3. Pick up the gear and get the job done.
