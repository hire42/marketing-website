Questions, answered

What can I list on Hire42?
Tools, trailers, vehicles, earthmoving equipment, scaffolding, compressors, generators — anything that sits idle between jobs and could be earning.
How do I get paid?
Payments are processed securely through Stripe. Your bank account is connected via Stripe Connect, so funds go directly to you after each completed rental.
Do I have to accept every request?
No. You stay in control — accept or decline every rental request, and set your own price and availability so rentals fit around your own jobs.
Is Hire42 a hire company?
No. Hire42 is a marketplace. We don't own the equipment — we connect the businesses and tradespeople who own it with the people nearby who need it.
