agree to the following principles and behaviors:

1. **Responsible Use**: I will use the rented equipment responsibly and in

equipment in any way that may cause damage or pose a safety risk.

2. **Care and Maintenance**: I will take proper care of the rented equipment

   laws, regulations, and safety standards while using the rented equipment. I
will obtain any necessary permits or licenses required for its operation, if
applicable.

5. **Timely Return**: I will return the rented equipment to the owner by the
   agreed-upon date and time. If any changes to the rental period are necessary,
I will communicate them with the owner and seek their approval.

6. **Feedback and Improvement**: I will provide honest and constructive feedback
